# Freedom Agent — Goals

## Paul's Vision of Freedom

### Primary Goals (Heaviest Weight)

1. **Time Freedom**
   - No fixed schedule or alarm clock
   - No meetings demanding presence at specific times
   - Work when he's most productive, not when the calendar says so

2. **Location Freedom**
   - Work remotely from anywhere in the world
   - No office, no commute, no tether to a physical location
   - The freedom to travel, relocate, or simply work from a café in Barcelona

3. **Autonomy**
   - No manager to report to
   - No one else's timeline to follow
   - Full control over his work and decisions

### Secondary Goals

4. **Passive Income**
   - Build toward streams that don't require active trading of time for money
   - Not the primary driver, but important for long-term security

## Why Now?

- Management changes made the job less enjoyable
- COVID shifted work to more desk-related tasks
- Lost the hands-on engineering work he originally loved
- Ready for a new chapter
