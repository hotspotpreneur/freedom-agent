## The Blend: What We're Creating

Your brand voice will be a powerful blend of:
- **Joe Vitale's hypnotic, gut-level pull** — emotional, feeling-based, mysterious
- **Frank Kern's direct response savvy** — strategic, result-driven, conversational

**The result:** Copy that feels like a conversation with a wise friend who happens to have a solution you'll love — and makes you want to act.

---

## Dr. Joe Vitale's Signature Techniques

### 1. Write From Good Feeling
- Don't sell — **entertain and illuminate**
- If it doesn't feel good to write, it won't feel good to read
- Write as if you're whispering a secret

### 2. The Hypnotic Formula
- **Curiosity gap** — Hint at something without giving it away
- **Embedded commands** — Soft commands disguised as normal sentences
- **Repetition with variation** — Say the same thing different ways
- **Sensory language** — Make readers see, feel, hear your words

### 3. Emotional Triggers
- Hope over fear
- Curiosity over logic
- Feel before think
- "What if..." thinking

### 4. Joe's Phraseology
- "The secret is..."
- "Here's why..."
- "You might be wondering..."
- "Imagine if..."
- "What if I told you..."

---

## Frank Kern's Signature Techniques

### 1. Intent-Based Branding
- Meet people where they are in their journey
- Mirror their desires back to them
- Make it feel like *they* thought of it

### 2. Conversational & Casual
- Writes like he's emailing a friend
- No stuffy corporate speak
- Short sentences. Punchy. Real.

### 3. Storytelling
- Share (or invent) relatable stories
- Use "what happened was..."
- Makes the reader the hero

### 4. Direct Response DNA
- Clear, specific call-to-action
- Results-oriented language
- "Here's what to do next..."

### 5. Frank's Phraseology
- "So here's the thing..."
- "Here's what works..."
- "Long story short..."
- "This is the part where..."
- "Bottom line..."

## PureForm Voice: The Perfect Blend

### Tone
- **Conversational** (Frank) but with **emotional depth** (Joe)
- **Confident** but never arrogant
- **Helpful** — we give first, sell second
- **Mysterious enough to intrigue** — "there's more..."

### Reading Level
- 8th grade — clear, punchy, no jargon
- Short sentences
- Paragraphs of 1-3 sentences max
- White space is your friend

### Emotional Palette
- Hope. Possibility. Transformation.
- "What if your health could be that simple?"
- "Imagine waking up and actually feeling good..."

### Don't Be
- "Woo-woo" (stay grounded in science)
- Salesy or pushy
- Academic or clinical
- Generic ("boost your health today!")

## Guidelines for All Copy

1. **Lead with feeling, back up with fact**
   - Emotion first, science supports

2. **Write ONE reader at a time**
   - "You" not "you guys"

3. **Cut every useless word**
   - If it doesn't pull or push, delete

4. **Read it out loud**
   - If you stumble speaking it, you stumble reading it

5. **Test, test, test**
   - Frank Kern tests headlines. So should we.
