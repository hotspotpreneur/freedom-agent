# PureForm Wellness — Site Menu Structure
## Brainstorm Options

---

## Option A: Simple 5-Pillar (Recommended)

**Primary Menu:**
- Home
- Shop Supplements
- Foods
- Wellbeing
- About

**Footer Menu:**
- Exercise
- Meditation
- Contact
- FAQ
- Privacy

**Why:** Keeps main nav clean, categories self-explanatory

---

## Option B: Mega Menu

**Primary Menu:**
- Home
- Shop (with dropdown: Supplements | Foods | Accessories)
- Exercise (with dropdown: Equipment | Routines | Recovery)
- Wellbeing (with dropdown: Sleep | Stress | Mental Clarity)
- Meditation (with dropdown: Apps | Gear | Guides)

**Footer:**
- About
- Contact
- Blog
- FAQ

**Why:** Good for stores with many products, but can feel cluttered

---

## Option C: Category-First

**Primary Menu:**
- Supplements
- Foods  
- Exercise
- Wellbeing
- Meditation

**Secondary (header top):**
- About
- Contact
- Search

**Footer:**
- Blog
- FAQ
- Privacy

**Why:** Every category visible at once — good for discovery
